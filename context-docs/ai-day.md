# R/Pharma genAI Day 2026

The third Annual R/Pharma genAI Day will be held virtually on **June 16, 2026**. This free single-day event will focus on examples of using generative and agentic artificial intelligence with an emphasis in the drug development space and clinical domain.

- Expo (virtual booth) submission form: <https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeTxjipa92YWmM6ZZhb2C6hFKmlB2b7eYaQ33lQZIIu-wuOPw/viewform>

## Virtual Expo

The event features a virtual exhibition hall open throughout the day. Open-source developers are invited to submit a booth if they have an R or Python package, app, AI agent or skill, or contribute to the community via a YouTube channel, blog, or podcast.
